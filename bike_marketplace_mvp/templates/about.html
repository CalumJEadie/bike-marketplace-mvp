About


Bikemarketplace is the easiest way to buy and sell used bicycles online.

Mass market auctions and classifieds are crippled by a a lack of trust, varying quality and no customer service. We want to put people back at the centre of buying and selling bikes.

  • Buy a real bike from real people. Every listing is linked to a personal profile so you know who you're dealing with and we let users add social media profiles and forum memberships. Our team verifies all sellers individually and ensure all bikes are not stolen.

  • Sell with ease. We want to make it as easy as possible for sellers to list their bike and get a fair price. We offer free photography for all listings and with some basic details, we will research and write up the specs of your bike.

  • Convenience. We offer free pick-up and delivery for all bikes in central London (even for test rides!) and all sales come with a discounted service from a trusted bike mechanic.
